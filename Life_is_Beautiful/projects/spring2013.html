Project1-Sharks and Fish

Description

In this project I implemented a simulation of an ocean containing sharks and fish. I also wrote code to convert an ocean into a run-length encoding and back. The ocean is rectangular, but the edges are connected together in a topological donut or torus. This means that the top (North) and bottom (South) edges are considered adjacent, so if I start at the top edge and go up, I'll be at the bottom edge (just like in the video game Asteroids). Similarly, the East and West edges are connected (just like in Pac Man).
